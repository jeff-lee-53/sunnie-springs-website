About the Development

A Community Designed Around Nature

Sunnie Springs is a 13-lot residential subdivision being developed in Pleasant View, Utah — a community known for its open landscapes, mountain views, and strong sense of neighborhood.

This is more than a subdivision. It's a chance to put down roots in a place where families grow, neighbors know each other, and the land itself is something to be proud of.

City Meetings

Your presence matters. Prospective buyers who attend Pleasant View City meetings help demonstrate real community support for Sunnie Springs. Here's how to be part of it.

No Meetings Currently Scheduled

Check back here for upcoming Pleasant View City meetings. We'll post dates, times, and locations as soon as they're announced so you can show up and show your support.

🌿 Why Your Presence at City Meetings Matters

  • Community support sends a strong, positive signal to city officials that this development is wanted and welcomed.
  • Prospective buyers who show up demonstrate genuine neighborhood interest — it helps build momentum for approval.
  • Meetings are open to the public — simply attending makes an impact, no speaking required.
  • You'll hear directly from city officials about the approval timeline and any outstanding questions.

Part of the Sunnie Springs Community

Sunnie Springs Farm

A separate — but deeply connected — piece of the Sunnie Springs identity. The Sunnie Springs Farm is a living, growing 2-acre farm in Pleasant View, Utah: 200+ plants and trees, 8 ponds, active irrigation, and a 3,000 sq ft greenhouse arriving fall 2026.

This is the spirit of Sunnie Springs made visible — a community rooted in the land, in growth, and in the good life that northern Utah makes possible.
